About

A quiet space for real food.

KIMIKO is a whole‑food recipe salon: pastel‑coloured on the surface, strict underneath. Every recipe follows the same rule — ingredients you would feel comfortable serving in a calm, bright kitchen, without hidden ultra‑processed extras.
